Access to Multiple Locations: Members can use different gyms and fitness centers within the network, giving them a range of workout options.

Class Passes: Many FitPass plans offer passes to group fitness classes, such as yoga, pilates, spinning, and HIIT.

Personal Training Sessions: Some plans may include a few personal training sessions or discounted rates on personal training services.

Wellness and Recovery Services: Amenities might also extend to wellness services like massage therapy, sauna, or steam rooms at participating locations.

Online Classes and Programs: Depending on the plan, there might be access to virtual fitness classes or workout programs that can be done from home.

Discounts and Offers: FitPass members might receive special discounts on additional services or products offered by partner facilities.
